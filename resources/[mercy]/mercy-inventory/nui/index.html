Keybinds (Defaults)

  • Ctrl + C Copies the item information you hover over.
  • Shift + Right Click Moves half of the stack to opposite inventory.
  • Ctrl + Right Click Moves one of the stack to opposite inventory.
  • Left Click Drag Item
  • Right Click Quick Move
  • Middle Click Use Item
  • Scroll Wheel While focussing on the "AMOUNT" field will increase / decrease value
  • Numbers 0 - 9 Will input the typed number.

Stores

Put in the amount you want to buy in the AMOUNT field and then drag the shop item into your inventory.

Crafting

Make sure you have enough items to craft what you want.

Item Durability

Almost all items will decay or get damage over time (including weapons). The background of each item will slowly decrease in height as it decays / takes damage more and more, then eventually fade to a reddish color. Once an item is fully destroyed, it will have a solid red background.
